Get in touch with Me

Hi there! My name is Sharadha, but most people call me Shar.

I love photography, food, music and anything that brings joy and a smile to my face. If your idea of preserving memories is a set of photots made up of emotions, colors, spontaneity, then do get in touch with me, because I feel the same way.

I love photographing people and I'm open to shooting pretty much anything ranging from weddings to babies. So if you like the pictures in my gallery, reach out to me and we'll plan a photo-date!

While doing a photoshoot, it is very important to design the photoshoot right, in order to get the best results. This is something I love doing and will go over with you before we start shooting.

When you do get in touch with me, let me know what the event is, how I can help plan / design the photoshoot, and what is the best way to get in touch.

Cheers!
